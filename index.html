HAPPY ❀ ❀


April032020
Here are some happy memories from my past as a means to -ever so slightly- help offset everything negative that is happening around the world right now.
+ finding sand dollars at the beach and thinking they were actually worth tons of money (when I was like 6 years old)
+ eating donuts by the beach in the summertime
+ getting chased by seagulls at the pier with friends because they zoned in on our sandwiches like flying arrows at a target
+ going out of my way to watch the fireworks in the freezing cold on the 4th of July
+ exploring city-views at different vantage points
+ hiking my favorite trail… twice in one year
+ seeing snowflakes land on window glass and melt a millisecond later
+ coming across a particularly satisfying monologue from a singularly unexpected source (anton ego from ratatouille :>)

❀ Sometimes, I feel as if the world is spinning a little too fast, that if it would just slow down a little,
then maybe I could just somehow... catch my breath. As I am growing older, the time just seem to fly by faster
and faster with each passing day- making my futile attempts at trying to grasp the present feel irreconcilably moot.
As I am digging up some of my happiest memories and typing it out for the world to see, I can’t help but think that all we
have is the present and that everything that isn’t the present, only robs us of what little that we have.
The hours, minutes, seconds that we spend with family and friends, the time we invest into exploring our identities, or
whatever else out there that makes you feel alive, is truly what gives meaning to our lives.
